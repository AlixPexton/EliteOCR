How to TRAIN EliteOCR

First run color calibration with about 3-5 Screenshots. It will adapt to your screenshot color and contrast.

Then start learning wizard. Delete user data on the first screen (if possible).

Go to second step. Add 1-5 screenshots and click on OCR. Then check every field and correct every error until you reach the end (the next button will become disabled).

Then you can go to next step, click on "Add those images..." and then click on training.

Wait 2-10 minutes (depending on your processor speed).

Voila! You trained EliteOCR and should have far less errors (if any at all).
